## Node Centrality
### Betweenness centrality
cv=其它节点间的短路径经过v节点的数目

### Closeness centrality
cv=1/(sum{v与其它节点的最短路径长度})

## Clustering Coefficient
ev=(周围节点相邻边数)/(理论可连总数)

## Graphlets
### Graphlet Degree Vector(GDV)

$A_{uv}^l$ uv间长度为l的路径数
